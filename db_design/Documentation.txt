Students Table:

Contains information about students, including their unique ID, name, date of birth, and the department they belong to.


Courses Table:

Stores information about courses, such as the course ID, name, credits, and the department offering the course.


Departments Table:

Represents different academic departments, with a unique department ID and the department name.


Grades Table:

Records the grades of students in various courses, linking student and course information through foreign keys.


Additional Notes:

Relationships: The DepartmentID in the Students and Courses tables establishes a relationship with the Departments table.
Data Types: Ensure that appropriate data types (e.g., VARCHAR, INT, DATE) are used for each column.
Indexing: Consider adding indexes on columns frequently used for querying, such as primary and foreign key columns.
